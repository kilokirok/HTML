How to make soy sauce chicken nuggets

Ingredients

  • Chicken nuggets
  • Any kinds of nuts

Ingredients for sources

  • Soy source
  • Sugar
  • Oligosaccharide
  • crushed garlic
  • Plum extract
  • Cooking wine
  • Water
Recipe
Steps Procedures Additional notes Time Ingredients for this step
One Fried chicken nuggets Pour cooking oil enough to soak about half of the chicken nuggets in the frying pan and fry them side by side 5 minutes each side Cooking oil, Chicken nuggets
Two Getting rid of oil Place the fried nuggets on a kitchen towel to remove oil out side Kitchen towel
Three Making sauce In a small bowl, add 3 spoons of thick soy sauce, 2 spoons of sugar, 3 spoons of oligosaccharide, 1 spoon of minced garlic, 1 spoon of plum extract, 2 spoons of cooking wine, and 3 spoons of water and mix it 1 minutes Soy sauce, sugar, oligosaccharide, minced garlic, plum extract, cooking wine, and water
Four Mixing nuggets with sauce Put the sauce in a recessed frying pan and heat it over high heat. When it starts to boil after a few seconds, lower the heat, add all the chicken nuggets, and mix them with a spatula 1~2 minutes
Five Add nuts according to your preference When the sauce is absorbed into nuggets, add the crushed nuts and mix 30 seconds Crushed nuts

Enjoy!
