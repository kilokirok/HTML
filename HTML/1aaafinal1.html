Why MSF

While doing missionary work, I saw people in California and Korea, where I thought there would not be many people who could not receive treatment, who could not receive proper medical treatment due to financial difficulties. It was hard to imagine how many people were suffering in developing countries. MSF is an organization of the medical people who wants to help those people.

What MSF do?

Médecins Sans Frontières brings medical humanitarian assistance to victims of conflict, natural disasters, epidemics or healthcare exclusion.

Where are them

In more than 70 countries, Médecins Sans Frontières provides medical humanitarian assistance to save lives and ease the suffering of people in crisis situations.
