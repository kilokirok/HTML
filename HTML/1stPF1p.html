Singapore

Singapore is the city country. Has three official langueses. This shows us a lot of cultures in this small country! You can find tradition cultures of India, China and Arab and also you can find very modern cultures!

Place to visit and how to enjoy Singapore

There are several places to visit! Check the list down bellow!

  1. Marina bay sands

    This is a 5 star hotel called "Marina Bay Sands". You can look down a nightscape and enjoy water pool at a height of 200 meters.

  2. Gardens by the bay

    This is Gardens by the bay! My favorit part of this place is light show at night! There are electric trees and they offers light show with good musics! And you can enjoy it with out ticket!

Foods in Singapore

As many cultures combind in the Singapore, there are a lot of good foods in Singapore from many cultures!

  2. Crabs
