Something to see along the way

  1. Opposite Traffic way

    Singapore has left hand traffics! It doesn't seem like a big deal, but you'll be surprised because it will make you very confused. And you also might feel reverse culture shock when you come back.

  2. Double-decker bus

    If you want to tour downtown, it will be the best choice! A Double-decker bus helps you to tour downtown in high sight!
